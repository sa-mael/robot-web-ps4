FABRICATION PROTOCOLS

The Arttous v75.4 platform relies on a distributed manufacturing model. We do not sell hardware; we provide the Digital DNA for you to synthesize it locally. The STL files provided below are optimized for FDM printing with a recommended layer height of 0.16mm to accommodate the new high-torque kinematics engine.

Material Compliance: While standard PLA is sufficient for kinetic testing, field deployment requires materials with higher glass transition temperatures. We strongly recommend Carbon Fiber PETG for the chassis to reduce flex during RTOS dual-core high-speed maneuvers, and TPU 95A for the footpads to maximize the friction coefficient on concrete surfaces.

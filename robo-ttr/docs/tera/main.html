About Us

Art in motion, technology in action

At Arttous, we blend biomechanics and AI to build a four-legged robot that masters any terrain.

Every mechanical part is 1:1 with real-world dimensions, built for 3D printing and real-life assembly. Logical design and precise tolerances ensure reliable performance in the field.

Fast and stable movement across diverse terrains.

PS4 Control

Low-latency operation using a DualShock controller.

Autonomous Memory

Remembers its path and returns to base if control is lost.

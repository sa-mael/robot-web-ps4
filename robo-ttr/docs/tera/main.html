About Us

Art in motion, technology in action

At Arttous, we blend cutting-edge biomechanics with advanced AI navigation to build a quadruped robot that masters the most challenging terrain. Our four-legged platform delivers unparalleled balance and agility, letting you traverse rocky slopes, muddy fields, and uneven surfaces with confidence.

Controlled via a PS4 DualShock interface, Arttous gives operators millisecond-level responsiveness and intuitive feedback, so you can focus on the mission—not the controls. And with our custom wireless protocol, latency stays under 50 ms even at 100 meters.

Quadruped Locomotion

Fast and stable movement across diverse terrains.

PS4 Control

Low-latency operation using a DualShock controller.

Autonomous Memory

Remembers its path and returns to base if control is lost.

Future Applications

Ideal for geological mapping, rescue missions, and archaeological exploration.

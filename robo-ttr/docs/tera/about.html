About Arttous

Arttous is a next-generation quadruped robot featuring precision servo-driven articulation for agile navigation across challenging landscapes. Powered by a generic ESP32 core and controlled via a low-latency PS4 DualShock interface, Arttous blends mechanical robustness with proprietary AI path-memory to ensure mission success.

Our Mission

We blend biomechanics, advanced kinematics, and rugged engineering to build robotic explorers that unlock new frontiers:

  • Geological Mapping — autonomously survey terrains and collect data.
  • Search & Rescue — penetrate disaster zones safely and guide teams.
  • Archaeological Exploration — navigate ruins and preserve fragile sites with precise movement.

Our Team

A multidisciplinary group of roboticists, software engineers, and field scientists united by a passion for exploration and innovation. Together, we push the limits of what legged robots can achieve in the real world using accessible, high-performance components.

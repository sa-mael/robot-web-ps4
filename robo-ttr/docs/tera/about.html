About Arttous

Arttous is a state-of-the-art quadruped robot designed for rapid, reliable navigation across challenging landscapes. Powered by a PS4 DualShock controller interface and proprietary AI path-memory, Arttous can retrace its steps and return home autonomously if connection is lost.

Our Mission

We blend biomechanics, advanced control algorithms, and rugged engineering to build robotic explorers that unlock new frontiers:

  • Geological Mapping — autonomously survey terrains and collect data.
  • Search & Rescue — penetrate disaster zones safely and guide teams.
  • Archaeological Exploration — navigate ruins and preserve fragile sites.

Our Team

A multidisciplinary group of roboticists, software engineers, and field scientists united by a passion for exploration and innovation. Together, we push the limits of what legged robots can achieve in the real world.
